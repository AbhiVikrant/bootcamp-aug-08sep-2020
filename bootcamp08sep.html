Home

GAKI, Global Asia Knowledge Initiatives, is a non-profit and non-political organisation working towards creating a dynamic knowledge ecosystem around the idea of Global Asia. GAKI’s short-and medium-term interventions include organizing seminars & conferences, workshops, lectures and online courses; offering consultancies; forging research and knowledge partnerships; setting up physical and online platforms for generation and transmission of knowledge; and, leading training and awareness sessions. In the medium-long term, the organization will aim to set up institutes, campuses and other related facilities. The organization will work in collaboration with various stakeholders—including educational institutions, research institutes, social and cultural organisations, think tanks, governments, embassies & diplomatic bodies, corporates & industries, aid agencies, international organisations, and civil and society organisations—globally, towards the fulfillment of its wider objectives.
